What is a backing plate?

A MyTagDesignAI backing plate mounts behind your state-issued license plate. The design area surrounds the tag, while the plate number, decals, and registration details remain visible.

FAQ

Is this a license plate cover?

No. MyTagDesignAI products are decorative backing plates designed to mount behind the state-issued license plate.

Will it block my license plate?

The product is designed so the tag number, registration decals, and plate information remain visible and unobstructed.

What material are you starting with?

We are starting with aluminum because it is lightweight, durable, weather-resistant, and cost-effective.
